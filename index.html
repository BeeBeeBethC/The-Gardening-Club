reasons to join us

clear your mind

Studies have shown that spending time in nature. Yes, that includes being outdoors in the garden! Can help tremendously to calm and reduce anxiety and help with depression by focussing and being present.


lower stress levels

By gardening more, results in stress reduction and improves well-being.


gardening?! a form of exercise?

Yes! By being outdoors and gardening for just 30 minutes per day has shown that gardening burns calories!


grow your own!

By growing your own fruit and vegetables, you can pick what you need, when you need it and the produce is fresh every time!


reduce loneliness

By joining The Gardening Club, reduce loneliness by increased social interaction!
