About Us

The Gardening Club was established in June 2020, running online only at the time through the use of video tutorials and Q & A sessions to help encourage people to be outside enjoying the fresh air in their own back gardens.


The private space currently used for online tutorials consists of four raised beds and two green houses on approximately one acre of land. Along with these are a variety of flowerbeds: A rose bed, low shrubbery, a camelia garden, two main seating areas as well as a variety of acers and fruit trees.


We also host Q & A coffee and catch up sundays via Zoom answering any queries that members may have. At the beginning of spring, children (and adults too!) get to learn how to plant their own sunflowers and enter a competition with prizes for the entries that manage to grow the biggest sunflower of the season!
